#  AI Assisted Sales Analytics Dashboard <img src="https://img.icons8.com/color/96/microsoft-excel-2019.png" width="50"/> 

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on building an interactive Sales Analytics Dashboard in Microsoft Excel to analyze business performance, monitor KPIs, track revenue trends, and identify product-wise sales insights.

The dashboard helps in understanding:
- Revenue and cost trends
- Product performance analysis
- Revenue contribution by retail stores
- Sales growth tracking
- KPI-based business reporting

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Handwash was the most sold product with the highest units sold.
- Future Retail contributed the highest revenue share among retail stores.
- Revenue trends fluctuated across different time periods.
- Cumulative revenue analysis showed overall business growth progression.
- Revenue and cost comparison helped identify business performance patterns.
